Factors for Fall Hikes

List of the most important factors for a fall hike

  1. Road safe to drive a Honda Fit
  2. Aspen groves at peak color
  3. 90 minute drive from Denver
  4. Elevation gain of at least 2000 feet
  5. 6 to 12 mile long hike

Potential Fall Hikes

Hikes MUST have the following:

  • Within a 90 minute drive from Denver
  • Safe road for a low clearance Honda Fit
  • Aspens groves in peak color

Link to the Abyss Trail to Helms Lake hike with details from Alltrails:

/trail/us/colorado/abyss-trail-to-helms-lake.

This is definitely a worthwile hike to do in the autumn, even with crowds and traffic. The hike starts of pretty easy, with a jawdropping view of the aspens 2.5 miles in. Miles 4 and 5 are steeper with switchbacks. There is a great view of Bierstadt and other peaks. It is close to Denver and easily accessible.
